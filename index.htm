Seeking a career to be a UX/Learning Experience Designer

I have a passion for technology, especially if it's being used as a tool to help people. My creativity and empathy for equity led me to design innovative solutions with visual designs. As a native English teacher in Japan, I was faced with students' mistaken belief that English is too difficult. I worked with Japanese teachers of English who followed a rigid curriculm with little flexibility. There was little emphasis on English communication. A foreign English teacher fills in this gap, but lacks teaching skills and strategies to successfully introduce Japanese learners to cultural diversity. I decided to create solutions. I developed a website called "Eigo Ganbare" to make learning English easier, practical, and engaging! Now that I'm in the States, my goal is to be a UX Designer with an interest in designing educational learning apps and/or be part of an accessibility design team to help people with learning difficulties. I'm also open to other opportunities, like visual/interaction design for desktop and mobile apps, and the design proccess of AR/VR or web-based app development.
