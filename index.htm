Seeking a career to be a UX Designer

I have a passion for technology, especially if it's being used as a tool to help people. My creativity and empathy involves innovating solutions with visual designs. As a native English Teacher in Japan, I was faced with students' attitude - in that they think English is too difficult. I worked with Japanese Teachers of English who must follow a rigid curriculm with little flexibility in how it is taught effectively. There is little emphasis on English communication. A foreign English teacher fills in this gap, but lack the teaching skills and ways to successfully introduce Japanese to cultural diversity. Because of those, I wanted to create solutions. So, I developed a website called "Eigo Ganbare" to make learning English easier, practical, and engaging! Now that I'm in the States, my goal is to be a UX Designer with an interest to design educational learning apps and/or be part of the accessibile design team to help people cope with learning difficulties. I'm also open to other opportunities, like the design proccess of AR/VR or web-based game development and making a website more accessible.
